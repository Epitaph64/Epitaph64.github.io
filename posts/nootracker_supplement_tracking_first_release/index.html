This is the first released version. This is for testing purposes primarily. I will update this post with Reddit discussion posts as they occur.

This software is designed to make it easier to track taken doses of Nootropic supplements. It also shows very simple half life decay evaluations based on the dosage event log, and can generate HTML reports or print to PDF, etc. for data backup/sharing purposes. More advanced features may be included in the future depending on the feedback, etc. but may be included in a paid product at some point if there's enough interest.
